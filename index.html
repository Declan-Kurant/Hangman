Fancy a game of hangman?

Instructions

To play, simply input the word to be guessed, press "new game" and then the other players may begin guessing. If you grow tired of your word choice, type a new word and press "new game" to reset. You may also attempt to solve the entire puzzle by inputting your guess and pressing "solve puzzle". But careful! You only get one try.
Be sure to input your letter guesses into the guess box.
